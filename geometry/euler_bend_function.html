Create a EulerBend curve.

:param degrees central angle in degrees :param radius_min radius minimum :param radius_eff radius effective choose either radius_min(imum) or radius_eff(ective) :param p radio of euler spiral in whole bend, 0 < p <= 1, when p = 1, there's no cirular part in the bend :param l_max max length of euler spiral in half bend, l_max is paired with radius_min choose either p or l_max, if both absent, p = 1 is assumed.
